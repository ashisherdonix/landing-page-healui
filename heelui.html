Bridging the Physiotherapy Gap

India faces a critical shortage of physiotherapists, with only 1 physiotherapist per 100,000 people compared to the WHO recommended ratio of 1:10,000. This leaves millions without access to proper rehabilitation care.

Healui.ai is here to transform this landscape. By empowering existing physiotherapists with AI-powered tools, we're multiplying their impact, enabling them to serve more patients effectively while maintaining the highest standards of care.

Our mission is simple: Make quality physiotherapy accessible to every Indian, regardless of their location or economic status.
